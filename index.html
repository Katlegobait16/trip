Milk & Cookies Festival Details

The Milk & Cookies Festival is Johannesburg's premier music and lifestyle festival, bringing together the best local and international artists for a day of unforgettable entertainment.

Festival Details

  • Date: January 10, 2026
  • Venue: Johannesburg Expo Centre, Nasrec
  • Time: 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM
  • Dress Code: Festival chic - comfortable yet stylish!
  • Tickets: Early bird tickets available from September 2025

Highlights

  • Multiple music stages featuring various genres
  • Food trucks and gourmet treats
  • Art installations and photo zones
  • Dance workshops and interactive experiences
  • Silent disco and after-parties

Day 3 Information

Sandton City Mall

Africa's largest shopping complex with over 300 stores including international brands like Zara, H&M, and Apple, plus local designers and specialty stores.

Highlights: Designer boutiques, food court, cinema, rooftop restaurants

Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM (Weekdays), 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Saturday), 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Sunday)

Gold Reef City

Theme park and entertainment complex built around a historic gold mine. Features thrill rides, family attractions, shows, and an underground mine tour.

Highlights: Anaconda roller coaster, Tower of Terror, 4D Theatre, Gold Mine Tour

Opening Hours: 9:30 AM - 5:00 PM (Weekdays), 9:30 AM - 6:00 PM (Weekends)

Ticket Price: R250-400 per person
